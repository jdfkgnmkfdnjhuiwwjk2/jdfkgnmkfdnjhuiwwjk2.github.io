FAQ

Common questions

Answers about the YegSirens map, call locations, privacy and incident information.

Why are medical calls moved?

Medical calls are moved to the closest intersection to protect the privacy of the individual(s) involved.

Why do some calls have no information?

YegSirens tries to provide details on every call when possible, but sometimes information is unavailable or cannot be confirmed.

Are the times always accurate?

No. Any times mentioned may not be accurate because of delays in transmission and reporting.

Is YegSirens official?

No. YegSirens is unaffiliated with emergency services, fire departments, municipalities or dispatch centres.

How do I request a call to be moved or removed?

Email yegsirens@gmail.com immediately with the call details and request.

Can this information be used against someone?

No. Information on the map is for public awareness and education only. It must not be used to harass, identify, target or interfere with anyone.
