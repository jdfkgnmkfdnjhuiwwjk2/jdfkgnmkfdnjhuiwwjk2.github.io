Edmonton Area Fire Response Map

Track fire department incidents across Edmonton, St. Albert and Parkland County.

YegSirens provides a public incident map showing fire calls, alarms, rescues, medical responses and other emergency activity from publicly accessible information.

View the Map How the Map Works

Privacy first

Medical calls and certain sensitive incidents are moved to the nearest intersection to help protect the privacy of the individual(s) involved.

Live Incident Map

Edmonton Fire Department response map

Use the map below to view incidents reported around Edmonton, St. Albert and Parkland County. Not every call may appear, and some calls may have limited details.

Important: Medical calls and more sensitive calls are placed at the closest intersection for privacy. Map markers should not be used to identify or target anyone involved in an incident.

Coverage Areas

Calls shown may include Edmonton, St. Albert and Parkland County fire department incidents.

Public Awareness

The map is provided for education and awareness for citizens in the city and county areas covered.

Map Details

Call notes may include apparatus names, dispatch information and YegSirens notes when details are available.
