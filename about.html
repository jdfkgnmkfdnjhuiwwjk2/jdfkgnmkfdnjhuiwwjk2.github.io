About

Public fire incident awareness for the Edmonton area

YegSirens helps citizens better understand fire department activity happening around their community.

What YegSirens does

YegSirens maintains an incident map showing selected fire department calls from Edmonton, St. Albert and Parkland County using publicly accessible information.

The goal is simple: provide public awareness, help people understand what types of calls are happening nearby, and make fire response information easier to view in one place.

Not an official emergency service

YegSirens is not affiliated with any fire department, emergency service, municipality, government agency or dispatch centre. For emergencies, always dial 911.

Respecting privacy

Medical calls and certain sensitive calls are moved to the nearest intersection to protect the privacy of the people involved.
