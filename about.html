About YegSirens

YegSirens is a volunteer project that maps fire department activity around Edmonton..

We believe in keeping the public informed while maintaining respect for privacy. That’s why some medical calls are only shown as approximate intersections, not exact addresses.

We’re not affiliated with any emergency service, and the information provided on this site and maps is for general awareness only and must not be used to harass, interfere with, or endanger any individuals, agencies, or operations.
